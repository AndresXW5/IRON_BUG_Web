About us

Our company is made up of Andrés Vilar y Josemi Peinado.

IRON BUG is the name of our company and website to facilitate and have a better management of our products and users. In it, you can download our video game, buy our products, visit our forum, view your profile...

Our video game is called Death Rush, is developed in Unity with C #. It consists of a sphere (the player) that has to advance through a linear level and avoid the obstacles that appear. The goal is to get the highest possible score and pass through the portals to advance between levels..
